About Me

My name is Teresa. I moved to Seattle a year ago from Taiwan. I am here on my adventure.I know nothing about Seattle before I moved here. I just picked a place on the map and decided to move. Nothing was wrong living in Taiwan. I just wanted to see the world before I am too afraid to leave my comfort zone.

My major in Taiwan was applied foreign language: English. Which apparently is not a foreign language here... I still struggle a lot with the cultural differences and English conversations. But this challenge is also what I wanted. I would rather challenge myself than live in my comfort zone. I believe in no pain, no gain!
